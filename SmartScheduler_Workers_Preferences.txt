SMARTSCHEDULER - WORKERS PREFERENCES DATASET

======================== ENGLISH VERSION ========================

Worker 1 I prefer working morning shifts because they fit better with my
daily routine. I am not available on Mondays and I would like to avoid
night shifts whenever possible. I would also prefer not to work on
Christmas Day. If possible, I would like to avoid being assigned to more
than two consecutive morning shifts.

Worker 2 I generally prefer afternoon shifts and I am available every
day of the scheduling period. I can work during holidays if necessary,
but I would like to avoid working more than five consecutive days. Night
shifts are acceptable, although I would prefer not to perform many of
them.

Worker 3 I enjoy working night shifts and I am available throughout the
entire scheduling period. I have no particular issues with holiday
assignments, but I would prefer not to work on New Year’s Eve. Whenever
possible, I would like most of my assignments to be during the night.

Worker 4 I prefer morning shifts and I am only available from Monday to
Friday. I am not available during weekends and I would like to avoid
both night shifts and holiday assignments whenever possible.

Worker 5 Afternoon shifts are my preferred option, although I am
flexible and can work any day of the month. I am willing to cover
holiday shifts if needed and I can occasionally work at night. However,
I would prefer not to be assigned too many consecutive afternoon shifts.

Worker 6 I prefer morning shifts and I am available every day except
Saturdays. Night shifts are particularly difficult for me, so I would
like to avoid them whenever possible. I would also appreciate having
January 1st as a day off.

Worker 7 I am comfortable working night shifts and can help cover them
whenever staffing requires it. I am also available during holidays and
weekends. However, I would prefer not to work too many consecutive night
shifts without being assigned other types of shifts in between.

Worker 8 I usually prefer afternoon shifts and I am available every day.
I would like to avoid working on Christmas Day and Saint Stephen’s Day
if possible. Night shifts are not my preferred choice, so I would like
to be assigned to them only when necessary.

Worker 9 Morning shifts are my preferred option and I am available
throughout the entire scheduling period. I can work during holidays if
required, but I would strongly prefer to avoid night shifts. I would
also like to avoid long sequences of consecutive morning shifts.

Worker 10 I prefer afternoon shifts and I am available every day. I can
occasionally work night shifts if needed, but I would rather avoid
working on December 24th and December 31st. Additionally, I would like
to avoid being scheduled for too many consecutive working days.
